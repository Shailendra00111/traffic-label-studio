Spec sheet

Built for labeling real streets, not toy datasets.

01
Two shape modes
Rectangles for clean-edged objects, polygons for irregular ones — click point-by-point and close the loop by clicking back on your start point.
02
Classes you define
Ships with Car, Bike, Bus, Truck, Auto, and Person — add your own on the fly, right from a popup after you draw a shape.
03
YOLO-ready export
Rectangles export as standard YOLO detection labels; polygons export as YOLO-seg format. One click bundles images, labels, and a data.yaml.
04
Per-user accounts
Local login system, so every saved label is stamped with who labeled it and when — useful the moment more than one person is annotating.
05
Runs offline
No account, no cloud upload, no internet connection required. Your images never leave your machine.
